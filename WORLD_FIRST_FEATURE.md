## 🎨 How It Works

### 1. Meal Completion Score
```
Analyzes your cart for:
- Protein content (from mains, starters)
- Carbohydrate balance (from rice, bread)
- Vegetable intake (from sides, salads)
- Healthy fats (from ghee-based items)

Returns: Score (0-100), Grade (A+ to F), Missing components
```

**Example:**
- Cart: Dal Makhani + Biryani
- Score: 72/100 (Grade B)
- Missing: "Add naan or roti for complete meal"

### 2. Smart Budget Optimizer
```
Finds cheaper alternatives that maintain meal quality:
- Same category items
- Lower price
- Good profit margin (win-win)

Returns: Potential savings, Alternative suggestions
```

**Example:**
- Original: Butter Naan (₹80)
- Alternative: Plain Naan (₹60)
- Savings: ₹20 (25% off)

### 3. Waste Prevention AI
```
Calculates total food weight:
- Average portion sizes per category
- Compares to ideal for number of people
- Warns if ordering too much

Returns: Waste risk (low/medium/high), Suggestions
```

**Example:**
- 2 people ordering 1.5kg of food
- Ideal: 1.4kg
- Risk: Low ✅
- Message: "Perfect portion size!"

### 4. Group Order Optimizer
```
Suggests ideal portions for sharing:
- Main Course: 1 per 2 people
- Bread: 1 per person
- Sides: 2 per group

Returns: Sharing score, Suggestions
```

**Example:**
- 4 people
- Ideal: 2 mains, 4 breads, 2 sides
- Current: 1 main, 2 breads, 1 side
- Suggestion: "Add 1 more main course"
